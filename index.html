CK Editor

  
So far CKEditor is my favorite, it supports templates and looks best on mobile. Unfortunately I have not been able to find a good solution for mobile friendly editor, they either do not have features we need, or are super expensive. With templates we can have a more standardized look and feel across all articles, or at least make that easier to do.
I tried a few otheres, but they sucked, so I didn't even bother putting them up here.
